📖 How to Play

⚔️ The Dungeon Awaits

Crypt Sweepers is a dungeon-crawling tile flipper. Each floor is a hidden grid — tap tiles to reveal what lurks beneath. Fight monsters, grab loot, and descend deeper before death finds you.

This is a Roguelike — death is part of the journey. Each run starts fresh, and the dungeon will humble you. Don't be discouraged; every death teaches you something new. Expect to die. A lot. The fun is in going a little further each time.

Between runs, visit the main menu to grow stronger for the next attempt:

🗡️
Heroes — unlock new characters with unique playstyles and abilities.
⚡
Active Abilities — purchase powerful skills like Slam, Arrow Barrage, and Blinding Light.
📜
Passive Upgrades — permanent bonuses that carry into every run — more HP, better damage, deeper pockets.
🛒
Gold Shop — spend gold earned in runs on upgrades and unlocks that persist forever.

🗺 The Grid

Every floor is a grid of hidden tiles. Tap any reachable tile to flip it. Only tiles adjacent to a revealed tile can be flipped.

📊 Your Resources

❤️
HP — reach 0 and it's over. Restore with potions, hearts, and camps.
🔵
Mana — spent on spells and abilities. Regenerates 1 per tile revealed.
⚔️
Damage — your melee range. Boosted by trinkets, level-ups, and upgrades.
🪙
Gold — spent at shops and the Gold Shop between runs.
✨
XP — gained by revealing tiles. Level up to gain mana and damage.

🎒 Backpack & Items

Your backpack holds up to 9 items. When it's full and you find something new, you'll be asked to swap or trash the new item.

⬜ Common — potions, tools, utility items
🔷 Rare — powerful passive trinkets
🟡 Legendary — game-changing relics
💡 Some trinkets trigger on equip — read the description before swapping!

🔮 Abilities & Spells

Each hero has unique abilities unlocked via upgrades. Abilities cost mana to use.

🌀
Spell — ranged magic attack. Tap an enemy to fire. Costs 3 mana.
🔨
Slam (Warrior) — hits all revealed enemies for area damage.
🏹
Arrow Barrage (Ranger) — rains arrows on a 3×3 area.
☠️
Poison Arrow (Ranger) — applies 3 turns of poison damage.

❓ Events

Event tiles trigger random encounters. Each run is different.

🧙
Goblin Merchant — spend gold on potions, tools, or a mystery relic.
🎲
Gambler — bet gold and roll dice. Roll 7+ to double your bet.
📦
Three Chests — pick one of three chests (common, rare, or legendary loot).
🔮
Trinket Trader — swap one of your trinkets for a random new one.
📜
Storyteller — narrative choice events with random outcomes.

💡 Pro Tips

🗺️ Reveal tiles strategically — uncover paths before fighting so you have escape routes.
⚡ One-shot fast enemies — they ambush you, so hit hard enough to kill in one blow.
🔑 Golden Keys — clear every enemy on a floor to earn a key. Spend them at the Magic Chest in Sanctuaries.
🏕️ Save your mana — mana regenerates slowly. Use spells on tough enemies, not weak ones.
💀 Boss on floor 5, then every 10 floors (15, 25, 35…) — use all your abilities and save mana for the fight.
🪙 Retreat wisely — retreating keeps 20% of your gold. Better than dying with full pockets.

🔮 Trinkets

Trinkets you have discovered on your runs. Tap an entry for details. Forged trinkets are created at the Sanctuary Forge.

Combine two trinkets into a powerful merged trinket. Both ingredients are consumed and a new item takes their place.
